Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Data We Collect

We collect personal information you provide when you contact us or book a consultation, including your name, phone number, email address, and any medical information you choose to share relevant to your enquiry or treatment.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to: respond to your enquiries, schedule and manage appointments, provide medical consultations and treatments, maintain clinical records as required by GMC guidelines, and communicate with you about your care.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the legal bases of: your consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR), performance of a contract for healthcare services (Article 6(1)(b)), and our legitimate interests in operating the clinic (Article 6(1)(f)). Special category health data is processed under Article 9(2)(h) for the provision of healthcare.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorised clinical and administrative staff only. We use encrypted systems and follow NHS information governance standards for clinical record-keeping.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to: access your personal data, request corrections, request deletion (subject to clinical record-keeping requirements), restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at altrimedical@gmail.com.

Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising networks. Any analytics tools used will be configured to protect your privacy.
