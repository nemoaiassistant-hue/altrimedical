Overview

IV drip therapy delivers vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and hydration directly into your bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system for near-instant absorption. At Altri Medical, our GMC-registered doctors administer a range of IV formulations designed to boost energy, support immunity, aid recovery, and promote overall wellness. Every IV drip is prescribed and supervised by a medical professional.

Benefits

  • Near-instant absorption — nutrients delivered directly to cells
  • 100% bioavailability vs 20-50% from oral supplements
  • Customisable formulations tailored to your health goals
  • Medical supervision ensures safety
  • Fast-acting results — many feel benefits within hours
  • Ideal for dehydration, fatigue, immune support, and recovery

The Procedure

  1. Consultation: Your doctor discusses your health goals and recommends the most suitable IV formulation.
  2. Preparation: Relax in a comfortable room while your doctor prepares your personalised IV drip.
  3. Administration: A small cannula is placed in your arm and the IV drip is administered over 30-60 minutes.
  4. Monitoring: Your doctor monitors you throughout the treatment.
  5. Aftercare: Cannula removed and you can go about your day immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an IV drip take?

Most take 30-60 minutes. Allow approximately 90 minutes for your full appointment.

Is IV therapy safe?

When administered by a qualified medical professional, IV therapy is very safe. All drips are prescribed and supervised by GMC-registered doctors.

How often can I have IV drips?

Frequency depends on your needs. Some patients have weekly sessions, others monthly maintenance. Your doctor will recommend an appropriate schedule.

Will I feel effects immediately?

Many patients report feeling benefits within hours — particularly increased energy. Full effects develop over 24-48 hours.
