Overview

At Altri Medical, we offer a comprehensive medical weight loss programme supervised by GMC-registered doctors. Our programme may include prescription medications such as Mounjaro (tirzepatide) and Ozempic (semaglutide) where clinically appropriate. Frequently Asked Questions

What are Mounjaro and Ozempic?

These are prescription medications originally developed for type 2 diabetes that work by mimicking hormones that regulate appetite and blood sugar, leading to reduced food intake and weight loss.

Am I eligible?

Generally prescribed for BMI over 30, or over 27 with weight-related health conditions. Your doctor will assess your individual case.

How much weight can I lose?

Clinical trials show 15-25% body weight loss over 12-18 months. Individual results vary. Our doctors focus on safe, sustainable weight loss.

Are there side effects?

Common side effects include nausea, diarrhoea, and fatigue, particularly when starting. These typically diminish as your body adjusts.

Do I have to inject myself?

Both are once-weekly subcutaneous injections. Your doctor provides full training on self-administration.
