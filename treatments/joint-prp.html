Overview

Joint PRP therapy uses the healing properties of your own blood to treat joint pain, tendon injuries, and early-stage osteoarthritis. At Altri Medical, our doctors combine concentrated PRP with specialised medical gel for targeted regenerative therapy. This approach harnesses natural growth factors to reduce inflammation, promote tissue repair, and improve joint function.

Benefits

  • Natural treatment using your body's own platelets
  • Reduces inflammation and pain in affected joints
  • Promotes tissue repair and cartilage regeneration
  • Can delay or avoid the need for surgery
  • Minimal side effects compared to corticosteroid injections
  • Suitable for knee, shoulder, hip, elbow, and ankle joints

The Procedure

  1. Consultation: Medical history review and examination. Imaging may be arranged.
  2. Blood draw & processing: Blood sample processed in centrifuge to concentrate PRP.
  3. Preparation: Treatment area cleansed and sterilised. Local anaesthetic applied.
  4. Injection: PRP gel combination injected into the affected joint using ultrasound guidance.
  5. Recovery: Brief rest at the clinic with post-treatment instructions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What conditions can joint PRP treat?

Osteoarthritis, tendonitis, ligament injuries, rotator cuff injuries, tennis elbow, golfer's elbow, and patellar tendinitis.

How many treatments are needed?

Typically 3 injections spaced 2-4 weeks apart, with maintenance every 6-12 months.

How long is recovery?

Light activities within 1-2 days. Strenuous activity avoided for 2-4 weeks.
