Overview

Our private doctor consultation is the starting point for your journey with Altri Medical. Whether you're interested in aesthetic treatments, concerned about a health issue, or simply want a thorough health assessment, our GMC-registered doctors provide the time, expertise, and attention that's often difficult to find in busy NHS settings. Every consultation is strictly confidential.

Benefits

  • Extended appointment time — no rushed consultations
  • Confidential and non-judgmental environment
  • GMC-registered doctors with NHS and private sector experience
  • Comprehensive assessment of your concerns
  • Honest, expert advice — including if treatment isn't right for you
  • Clear explanation of all options with transparent pricing

The Procedure

  1. Booking: Schedule by phone, email, or website. Flexible times including evenings and Saturdays.
  2. Consultation: In-depth discussion of concerns, medical history, and goals.
  3. Assessment: Thorough assessment with explanation of all available options.
  4. Treatment plan: Personalised plan created if treatment is appropriate.
  5. Follow-up: Ongoing support and follow-up appointments as needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a consultation?

Standard consultation is 30 minutes. Extended consultations available for complex cases.

What can I discuss?

Any aesthetic, health, or wellness concern — aesthetic treatments, weight management, hair loss, skin concerns, fatigue, and more.

How much does it cost?

The consultation fee is deducted from the cost of any subsequent treatment. Contact us for current pricing.

Is everything confidential?

Absolutely. All consultations are strictly confidential in accordance with GMC guidelines and GDPR.

Can I get a same-day appointment?

We try to accommodate same-day requests where possible. Call us to check availability.
