Overview

Dermal fillers are gel-like substances injected beneath the skin to restore lost volume, smooth lines, and enhance facial contours. At Altri Medical, we exclusively use premium hyaluronic acid (HA) fillers — the gold standard in aesthetic medicine. HA is a substance naturally found in your body, making these fillers biocompatible and reversible. Our doctors use advanced injection techniques to deliver subtle, harmonious results.

Benefits

  • Instant visible results with continued improvement as the filler integrates
  • Reversible — HA fillers can be dissolved if needed
  • Premium products from leading manufacturers
  • Natural-looking results that enhance rather than change your appearance
  • Versatile treatment for lips, cheeks, jawline, nose, and under-eye areas
  • Results typically last 6-18 months

The Procedure

  1. Consultation: Your doctor discusses your goals and recommends the most suitable filler type.
  2. Preparation: A topical numbing cream may be applied. Many premium fillers also contain lidocaine.
  3. Treatment: The doctor carefully injects the filler using fine needles or cannulas, sculpting and shaping as they go.
  4. Sculpting: The treated area is gently massaged to ensure smooth, even distribution.
  5. Aftercare: Detailed post-treatment instructions are provided.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are dermal fillers safe?

Yes. Hyaluronic acid fillers have an excellent safety profile and are fully reversible. All treatments are performed by GMC-registered doctors.

Do lip fillers look natural?

When administered by an experienced doctor, lip fillers can look completely natural. Our approach is always enhancement, not transformation.

How long do fillers last?

Lip fillers typically last 6-9 months, while cheek and jawline fillers can last 12-18 months.

Is the treatment painful?

Most patients experience only mild discomfort. We use fillers containing lidocaine and can apply numbing cream beforehand.

What areas can be treated?

Common areas include lips, cheeks, smile lines, jawline, chin, nose, under-eye hollows, and temples.
