Overview

Altri Medical offers confidential sexual wellness treatments for both men and women. The P-Shot (Priapus Shot) and O-Shot (Orgasm Shot) use Platelet-Rich Plasma from your own blood to improve sexual function, sensitivity, and intimate health. These treatments are performed by our GMC-registered doctors in a strictly confidential, non-judgmental environment.

Benefits

  • Uses your own blood — natural regenerative treatment
  • P-Shot: May improve erectile function and sensation in men
  • O-Shot: May improve arousal and lubrication in women
  • Strictly confidential environment
  • Performed by GMC-registered doctors
  • Minimal downtime and discomfort

The Procedure

  1. Consultation: Confidential discussion about your concerns, medical history, and goals.
  2. Blood draw & PRP preparation: Blood sample processed in centrifuge.
  3. Numbing: Topical or local anaesthetic applied.
  4. Treatment: PRP injected into targeted areas using fine needle.
  5. Aftercare: Post-treatment instructions. Normal activities within 24-48 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the P-Shot?

A regenerative treatment for men using PRP injected into the penis to improve blood flow, tissue health, and sensitivity.

What is the O-Shot?

A regenerative treatment for women using PRP injected into vaginal and clitoral areas to improve arousal and lubrication.

Is everything confidential?

Absolutely. All consultations and treatments are strictly confidential under medical confidentiality guidelines and GDPR.

How soon will I see results?

Some improvement within 2-3 weeks, optimal results over 3-6 months.
