Overview

Polynucleotides are one of the most exciting advances in regenerative aesthetics. Derived from highly purified DNA fragments, these bioactive molecules work at the cellular level to stimulate tissue repair, boost collagen and elastin production, and improve skin hydration. Unlike traditional fillers that simply add volume, polynucleotides actively regenerate your skin — improving quality, texture, and tone from the inside out.

Benefits

  • Cellular-level skin regeneration — treats root causes of ageing
  • Boosts collagen and elastin production
  • Deep hydration that improves skin texture and radiance
  • Reduces fine lines, pigmentation, and uneven tone
  • Accelerates skin healing and reduces inflammation
  • Long-lasting results with a recommended course of 3-4 treatments

The Procedure

  1. Consultation: Your doctor assesses your skin and recommends a treatment plan.
  2. Preparation: Treatment areas are cleansed and numbing cream may be applied.
  3. Treatment: Polynucleotide solution is injected using mesotherapy technique — small, precise injections across the treatment zone.
  4. Soothing: A soothing mask or serum is applied post-treatment.
  5. Aftercare: Detailed instructions to maximise results.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many treatments do I need?

A course of 3-4 treatments spaced 2-4 weeks apart is typically recommended, with maintenance every 3-6 months.

How is this different from fillers?

Fillers add volume; polynucleotides improve overall skin quality from within by stimulating your skin's own repair mechanisms.

Is there downtime?

Mild redness and swelling for 24-48 hours. Most patients return to normal activities the next day.

When will I see results?

Some improvement in hydration and glow immediately. Full effects develop over 4-8 weeks as collagen production increases.
