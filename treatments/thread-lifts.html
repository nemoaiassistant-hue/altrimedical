Overview

Thread lifts are a minimally invasive alternative to traditional surgical facelifts. At Altri Medical, our GMC-registered doctors use premium absorbable threads — including PDO (Polydioxanone) and PLLA (Polylactic Acid) — to gently lift and reposition facial tissue. The procedure also triggers your body's natural collagen response, meaning results continue to improve over the months following treatment.

Benefits

  • Immediate lifting effect with continued improvement over 3-6 months
  • Stimulates natural collagen production for lasting skin rejuvenation
  • Minimal downtime — most patients return to normal activities within 1-2 days
  • Performed under local anaesthetic for comfort
  • No visible scarring — threads are inserted through tiny entry points
  • Results can last 12-18 months depending on the thread type used

The Procedure

  1. Consultation: Your doctor assesses your facial anatomy, discusses your goals, and determines the optimal thread placement strategy.
  2. Preparation: The treatment area is cleansed and a local anaesthetic is applied to ensure your comfort.
  3. Thread insertion: Using a fine needle or cannula, absorbable threads are carefully placed beneath the skin to lift and reposition sagging tissue.
  4. Sculpting: The doctor gently adjusts the threads to achieve the desired lift, contouring the face for a natural, balanced result.
  5. Aftercare: A brief recovery period of 15-30 minutes, after which you can go home with detailed aftercare instructions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a thread lift take?

The procedure typically takes 45-90 minutes depending on the number of areas being treated. Most patients are in and out of the clinic within 2 hours.

Is a thread lift painful?

The procedure is performed under local anaesthetic, so you should not feel pain during treatment. Some mild discomfort, swelling, or bruising may occur afterwards.

How long do thread lift results last?

Results vary depending on the thread type used, but most patients enjoy visible lifting for 12-18 months. The collagen-stimulating effects provide ongoing improvement.

Am I suitable for a thread lift?

Thread lifts are ideal for patients with mild to moderate skin laxity who want a natural lift without surgery. The treatment works best for patients aged 30-60.

What is the recovery like?

Most patients experience mild swelling, tenderness, and slight bruising for 2-5 days. You should avoid strenuous exercise for 1-2 weeks.
