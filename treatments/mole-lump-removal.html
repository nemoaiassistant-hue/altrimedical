Overview

At Altri Medical, our GMC-registered doctors perform mole, cyst, skin tag, and lump removal using established surgical techniques in a clean, sterile clinical environment. Whether for cosmetic reasons, comfort, or peace of mind, our doctors can assess, advise, and remove unwanted skin lesions with precision and care. All removed tissue can be sent for histological analysis if required.

Benefits

  • Performed by GMC-registered doctors with surgical experience
  • Sterile clinical environment for optimal safety
  • Option for histological analysis of removed tissue
  • Minimal scarring with careful surgical technique
  • Quick procedures — most completed in 20-30 minutes
  • Both cosmetic and medical removals available

The Procedure

  1. Consultation: Your doctor examines the lesion and recommends the most appropriate removal method.
  2. Preparation: Treatment area cleaned and local anaesthetic administered.
  3. Removal: Surgical excision for deeper moles, shave removal for raised lesions, or cryotherapy for skin tags.
  4. Closure: Surgical excisions closed with fine sutures to minimise scarring.
  5. Aftercare: Detailed wound care instructions provided.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I have a scar?

Any surgical procedure leaves some form of scar, but our doctors use meticulous techniques to minimise scarring. The resulting scar is typically much smaller and less noticeable than the original lesion.

Can the removed mole be tested?

Yes. All removed tissue can be sent for histological analysis. This is recommended for any mole that has changed in size, shape, or colour.

How long does it take?

Most removals take 20-30 minutes. Allow approximately 45 minutes for your full appointment.

Is it painful?

The procedure is under local anaesthetic. Mild discomfort afterwards can be managed with over-the-counter pain relief.

Can skin tags be removed without surgery?

Yes. Small skin tags can be removed using cryotherapy (freezing), which requires no stitches.
