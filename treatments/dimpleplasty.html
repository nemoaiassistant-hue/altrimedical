Overview

Dimpleplasty is a minor surgical procedure that creates natural-looking dimples on the cheeks. The procedure works by creating a small attachment between the cheek muscle and the overlying skin, mimicking the natural anatomy that creates dimples in people who have them genetically. At Altri Medical, our doctors perform this procedure with meticulous precision to achieve symmetrical, natural-looking results.

Benefits

  • Permanent result — dimples visible when smiling after settling
  • Minimally invasive procedure under local anaesthetic
  • Quick procedure — typically 30-45 minutes
  • No visible external scarring
  • Highly customisable — position and depth tailored to your preferences
  • Excellent patient satisfaction rates

The Procedure

  1. Consultation: Your doctor discusses the desired position, depth, and size of dimples.
  2. Anaesthetic: A local anaesthetic is administered to numb the treatment area.
  3. Procedure: A small incision is made inside the cheek to create a controlled attachment between the muscle and inner skin.
  4. Sculpting: The dimple is carefully shaped for symmetry and natural appearance.
  5. Closure: The internal incision is closed with dissolvable sutures.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is dimpleplasty permanent?

Yes. Initially dimples may be visible at rest, but within a few weeks they typically settle to appear naturally when you smile.

Does it leave a scar?

The incision is made inside the cheek, so there is no visible external scarring.

How long is recovery?

Mild swelling for 3-5 days. Most patients return to work within 1-2 days. Full healing takes 4-6 weeks.

Is it painful?

The procedure is under local anaesthetic. Afterwards, mild discomfort can be managed with over-the-counter pain relief.

Can I choose where my dimples go?

Yes. Your doctor will discuss ideal positioning based on your facial anatomy and preferences.
