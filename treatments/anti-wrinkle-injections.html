Overview

Anti-wrinkle injections are one of the most popular and well-researched aesthetic treatments in the world. At Altri Medical, our GMC-registered doctors use clinically approved formulations to gently relax the muscles responsible for dynamic wrinkles — the lines that form when you frown, squint, or smile. The result is a smoother, more refreshed appearance that looks entirely natural.

Benefits

  • Clinically proven treatment with over 20 years of medical use
  • Quick procedure — typically completed in 15-20 minutes
  • Natural-looking results that preserve your facial expressions
  • Effective for forehead lines, frown lines, and crow's feet
  • Preventive benefits — regular treatment can slow new wrinkle formation
  • Results typically last 3-4 months

The Procedure

  1. Consultation: Your doctor assesses your facial anatomy and discusses your aesthetic goals.
  2. Marking: The injection sites are carefully mapped based on your unique facial anatomy.
  3. Treatment: Using a very fine needle, small amounts are injected into the targeted muscles. Most patients describe the sensation as a brief pinch.
  4. Review: Your doctor checks the treatment areas and provides aftercare advice. A follow-up may be scheduled at 2-4 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Botox safe?

Yes. Anti-wrinkle injections have been used medically for over 20 years with an excellent safety profile when administered by qualified practitioners.

How quickly will I see results?

Results typically begin to appear within 3-5 days, with the full effect visible at 10-14 days after treatment.

Will I look frozen or unnatural?

No. Our doctors use precise dosing techniques to soften wrinkles while preserving your natural facial expressions.

How long do results last?

Results typically last 3-4 months. Regular treatments can extend duration as targeted muscles gradually weaken.

Can it be used preventively?

Yes. Many patients in their late 20s and early 30s choose preventive treatment to slow the formation of expression lines.
