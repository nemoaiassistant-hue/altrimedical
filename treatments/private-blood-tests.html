Overview

Private blood testing at Altri Medical gives you fast access to comprehensive diagnostic panels without NHS waiting times. Our GMC-registered doctors select the most appropriate tests based on your health concerns and provide expert interpretation with clear, actionable guidance. Whether you want a general health check, hormone panel, or something more specific, we offer a range of packages.

Benefits

  • Fast results — typically within 24-72 hours
  • Expert clinician interpretation — not just numbers on a page
  • Wide range of panels including hormones, vitamins, and specialised markers
  • No NHS referral required
  • Confidential consultation to discuss results
  • Same-day blood draw in a comfortable clinical environment

The Procedure

  1. Consultation: Discuss your health concerns and your doctor recommends appropriate test panels.
  2. Blood draw: Simple blood draw performed by our trained medical professional — approximately 5-10 minutes.
  3. Processing: Sample sent to a UKAS-accredited laboratory for analysis.
  4. Results & interpretation: Doctor reviews results and arranges a follow-up to explain findings.
  5. Action plan: If results require attention, your doctor discusses next steps.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get results?

Most results within 24-72 hours. Your doctor contacts you as soon as results are in.

What tests do you offer?

Full blood count, liver/kidney function, thyroid, hormones, vitamin D, B12, iron, cholesterol, HbA1c, and more.

Do I need a GP referral?

No referral needed. You can book directly with us.

How much do blood tests cost?

Basic screening from £100, comprehensive panels from £150-£400. Your doctor discusses costs before any tests are arranged.

Do I need to fast?

Some tests require fasting (8-12 hours). Your doctor will advise when booking.
