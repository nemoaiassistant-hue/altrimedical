Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all treatments performed by qualified doctors?

Yes. Every treatment at Altri Medical is performed exclusively by GMC-registered, UK-licensed doctors with extensive NHS surgical, urological, and aesthetic experience. We never use technicians or non-medical practitioners.

Where is the clinic located?

Altri Medical is located at 35a Stamford New Road, Altrincham, WA14 1EB. We serve patients from Altrincham and the wider Cheshire area including Sale, Hale, Wilmslow, Knutsford, and Greater Manchester.

Do you offer weight loss treatments like Mounjaro and Ozempic?

Yes, we offer medical weight management programmes including Mounjaro and Ozempic where clinically appropriate. These are prescribed following a thorough consultation with our doctor to ensure suitability and safety.

How do I book a consultation?

You can book a consultation by calling us on 0161 531 7727, emailing altrimedical@gmail.com, or using the booking form below. We offer confidential 1-to-1 consultations with our medical team.

What is PRP treatment and how does it work?

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) treatment uses concentrated platelets from your own blood to stimulate tissue regeneration. We offer PRP for hair rejuvenation, joint repair, and sexual wellness (P-Shot and O-Shot). It is a safe, natural treatment performed by our medical doctors.

Is there parking available at the clinic?

The clinic is located on Stamford New Road in Altrincham town centre, with several public car parks and on-street parking nearby. Altrincham Metrolink and train station are also within walking distance.
