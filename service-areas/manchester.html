Getting to Altri Medical from Manchester City Centre

The journey from Manchester to Altrincham is one of the most straightforward in Greater Manchester:

  • By tram (recommended): The Altrincham Metrolink line runs direct from Piccadilly, Piccadilly Gardens, St Peter's Square, Deansgate-Castlefield, and Cornbrook. The journey to Altrincham station takes approximately 30 minutes, with trams running every 6–12 minutes throughout the day. From Altrincham station, our clinic on Stamford New Road is a relaxed 3-minute walk.
  • By car: Approximately 30 minutes via the M56 (junction 7) or the A56 through Sale. During rush hour, allow 40–45 minutes. Parking is available at nearby George Street car park and in Altrincham town centre.
  • By train: Northern Trains run regular services from Manchester Piccadilly to Altrincham station, with a journey time of around 25 minutes.

Many of our Manchester patients combine a clinic visit with a trip to Altrincham's award-winning market, independent shops, and restaurants — making the journey well worthwhile.
